The Region

Your Gateway to the Salzkammergut

The Salzkammergut is one of Austria's most enchanting landscapes — a tapestry of turquoise lakes, dramatic Alpine peaks and centuries of history. From Thalgau you are ideally placed to discover it all at your own pace.

⛰

Mountains

Lace up your hiking boots and explore alpine trails with breathtaking panoramas. The surrounding peaks offer routes for every level, from gentle hillside walks to challenging summit hikes. In winter, world-class ski areas are within easy reach.

🏊

Lakes

Swim in crystal-clear glacial waters, kayak on the Mondsee or Wolfgangsee, or simply relax on a lakeshore terrace with a coffee. The lakes of the Salzkammergut are a short drive away and unforgettable at every season.

🏛

Culture

Salzburg — birthplace of Mozart and a UNESCO World Heritage City — is just 20 minutes away. The fairy-tale village of Hallstatt, Bad Ischl and the imperial legacy of the Habsburg summer retreat are all within easy reach.
